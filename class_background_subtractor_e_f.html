Detailed Description

Exponential forgetting based Background/Foreground Segmentation Algorithm.

The class implements the exponential forgetting (EF) background subtraction. The background image is model with a Gaussian distribution, whose mean and covariance are calculated via EF formulas. The covariance matrix are taken isotropic for simplicity. The foreground image is obtained by thresholding Mahalanobis distance from the image to the mean.
